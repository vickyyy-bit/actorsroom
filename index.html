About Harshal & Actor's Room

Harshal is a seasoned actor and acting coach with over 15 years of experience in theater, film, and television. His journey began at the prestigious National School of Drama, and since then he has performed in numerous critically acclaimed productions.

The Actor's Room was founded in 2015 with a vision to create a nurturing space where aspiring actors can develop their craft under expert guidance. Our approach combines traditional techniques with innovative methods tailored to each student's unique potential.

What sets us apart is our commitment to not just teaching acting, but cultivating artists who can bring depth, authenticity, and emotional truth to their performances.
